Frequently asked questions

How do I connect my bank accounts?

In the app, choose to link an account and select your institution. Bank and card accounts connect securely through Plaid — you log in directly on Plaid’s secure screen, and we never see or store your bank credentials. To include your Apple Card, Apple Cash, or Apple Savings, grant access when prompted; that data is read securely on your device through Apple’s FinanceKit. You can disconnect any account at any time in the app, and revoke Apple Card access in iOS Settings.

How is my daily safe-to-spend calculated?

mybudget starts with the money you actually have, sets aside what’s already spoken for — your upcoming bills and savings goals — and spreads the rest evenly across the days until your next paycheck. That daily slice is your safe-to-spend. Spend less today and the remaining days grow a little; spend more and they shrink. The number updates as new transactions come in, so it always reflects what’s genuinely free to spend.

How do I add or remove fixed costs?

mybudget automatically detects most of your recurring bills and subscriptions from your linked accounts. Open the Bills section to review them — you can confirm or remove any the app found, and manually add anything it missed. You can edit or delete a bill there at any time, and your daily number updates to match.

How do I delete my account and data?

You can delete your account and associated data at any time directly in the app, under Settings. This removes your personal and financial data from our active systems (any residual copies in backups are overwritten within a limited period). If you’d rather not delete your whole account, you can also disconnect individual linked accounts instead.
