How it works

01

Connect your accounts

Link your bank accounts and cards so mybudget knows what's coming in and what's already committed.

02

Set your fixed costs

Rent, subscriptions, recurring bills — things already spoken for. mybudget subtracts them automatically.

03

See your number

What's left, divided by days remaining. One clean number. That's what you have to spend today.

04

Live within it

No willpower required — just check your number. The math does the hard part.
