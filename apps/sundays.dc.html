Sundays

A marketplace for worship musicians and churches.

Connecting worship musicians with churches and worship gigs. Real bookings, real payments — built for the people who make Sunday happen.

View on the App Store ↗ iOS

How it works

01

Create your profile

Musicians list their instruments, availability, and experience. Churches describe their worship style and needs.

02

Find the right match

Churches browse and book musicians. Musicians discover gigs that fit their schedule and calling.

03

Book and confirm

Bookings are confirmed in-app. Everyone knows the details — no back-and-forth over email.

04

Get paid

Payments are handled through the app — real money, on time, every Sunday.
